DLSS

DLSS is a new AI rendering technology that uses the power of NVIDIA RTX GPUs to deliver up to 2X the performance of traditional rendering techniques in games and up to 6X the performance in ray tracing. DLSS is available in GeForce Experience and is supported in over 50 games.
